Welcome to my CV, where I record where and what I sing!

This is an active and growing collection - so far I have input most everything from the present (2018) back to about mid-2010. There is more data entry to come!

FAQ

Q: What is this exactly?

A: This is a searchable, queryable history of everything I have ever sung. Or at least it will be once I get a lot more data entry done.

Q: Why are you doing this? What is this for?

A: I realized that I was getting bad at answering the question "Have you done <this piece> before?" And I find it quite fun to look back at all the awesome opportunities I have had!

Q: I don't want to appear in your database. How do I get out?

A: Because of the login requirement, this website shouldn't show up for any web searches for your name, so hopefully being listed here is pretty harmless! I am more than happy to remove you or edit your name to a pseudonym on request regardless though. Just use the contact form to reach out to me with your desired action.

Q: I want one of these for myself! How do I get one?

A: Just kidding, no one has asked this question.
